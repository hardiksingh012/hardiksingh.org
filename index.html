Case Study

Real project, real results

Google Ads Agency · USA

2,500 verified records across 5 industries & 4 US cities

A digital marketing agency needed a clean B2B prospect list for their Google Ads campaigns targeting local service businesses - HVAC, plumbing, dental, legal, and real estate - across New York, Chicago, Houston, and Los Angeles.

I built a multi-source scraper pulling from Google Maps and supplementary directories, deduplicated the dataset, verified phone formats, and flagged missing emails for secondary enrichment. Final delivery: a segmented Excel workbook, one tab per industry, CRM-ready.
